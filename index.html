Home


Welcome to my personal website. I am a UC San Diego Chancellor's Associates Scholar graduating in the spring of 2025 with a B.S in Cognitive Psychology and a minor in Cognitive Science. I am interested in research topics in the Cognitive and Social Psychology fields. I am specifically interested in social norms, problem solving, decision making, and human learning. I have been a research assitant at the Social Cognitive Development Lab at UCSD under Dr. Gail D. Heyman since the summer of 2023 conducting research on attitudes and interactions between people and ChatGPT.
