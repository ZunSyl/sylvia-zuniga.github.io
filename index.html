Home


Welcome to my personal website. I am a UC San Diego Chancellor's Associates Scholar graduate with a B.S in Cognitive Psychology and a minor in Cognitive Science. I am interested in research topics in the Cognitive and Social Psychology fields, including social norms, problem solving, decision making, and human-computer interaction. I have been a research assistant at the Social Cognitive Development Lab at UCSD supervised by Dr. Gail D. Heyman since the summer of 2023 conducting research on attitudes and interactions between people and ChatGPT. I am currently working at Driven Data as an Orthographic Transcription Team Lead at Driven Data and as an Admin Intern at the non-profit Creating S.P.A.C.E.
